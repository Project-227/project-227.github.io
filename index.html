About Project 227

A student-led initiative creating inclusive career pathways in Carmel, Indiana.

Our Mission

Project 227 supports families, schools, and communities in creating inclusive career pathways for individuals with special needs. In 2024, only 22.7% of individuals with disabilities who were eligible to work were employed. Our mission is to increase awareness, share opportunities, and build connections that lead to long-term independence.

Keynote Speakers

Ted Brown

Former VP, Toyota Motor Manufacturing Indiana

Ted Brown is a retired Vice President of Toyota Motor Manufacturing Indiana who traveled from Evansville to participate. During his career, Brown advocated for inclusive hiring and workplace accessibility. As the parent of an adult child with an intellectual disability, he effectively connected his personal experiences with his professional background.

Gordon Homes

Financial Advisor, WestPoint Financial Group

Gordon Homes is a Financial Advisor who focuses on helping families achieve long-term financial security. As a Special Care Planner and parent of a child with special needs, he specializes in lifelong care planning, including public benefits navigation, Special Needs Trusts, ABLE accounts, and guardianship considerations.
