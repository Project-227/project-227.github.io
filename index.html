About Project 227

A student-led initiative creating inclusive career pathways for individuals with special needs.

Our Mission

Project 227 is dedicated to supporting families, schools, and communities in creating inclusive career pathways for individuals with special needs. Our mission is to increase awareness, share opportunities, and build connections that lead to long-term independence.
